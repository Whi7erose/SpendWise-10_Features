How corporate tracking works
1

Corporate Card SMS Auto-Parsed

SpendWise detects corporate card identifiers in your bank SMS and routes those transactions to your corporate expense pool automatically.

2

Auto-Categorised by Type

Fuel, Meals, Accommodation, Business Travel, Entertainment each mapped to an IRD-recognised expense type for full tax deductibility.

3

Employee Reviews & Marks Reimbursable

Review auto-categorised items, mark reimbursable expenses, attach OCR receipts, and add a business purpose note all in one screen.

4

One-Click IRD Claim Generation

PDF generated in Inland Revenue Sri Lanka format VAT breakdown, business purpose, digital signature field emailed directly to Finance/HR.

Traceability
FR-10 UC5 Corporate Employee PESTEL

Implements FR-10 and UC5. Serves the Corporate Employee persona identified in Links to PESTEL Government Digital Push: Sri Lanka IRD's digitalisation of tax submissions makes this a government-aligned feature.
