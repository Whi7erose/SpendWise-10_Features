Feature 01 Auto SMS Tracking

Your Expense Feed Fills Itself

SpendWise reads every bank SMS notification and automatically builds your expense log. No typing, no forms your spending history appears the moment a transaction happens.

1

Open the Expense Feed tab. You will see a live list of all recent transactions, already sorted by date with merchant icons.

2

Read the coloured category badges Groceries (green), Dining (yellow), Fuel (blue), Transport (purple), Shopping (red). These are applied automatically.

3

Tap any row to open the full transaction detail merchant address, bank name, and which budget category was charged.

4

On the prototype page, click + Simulate SMS to watch a new transaction slide into the feed in real time.

Feature 02 Pre-Purchase Alert

Alerts Before You Spend

When you walk near a supermarket, SpendWise checks if there is a cheaper store nearby and sends you a saving alert before you tap your card.

1

Walk within 150m of a store SpendWise detects this automatically using your phone's location service.

2

Receive the push notification it shows you the cheaper nearby store and the exact LKR amount you will save on your usual basket.

3

Tap "Go to Cargills" to get walking directions to the cheaper store, or tap "Stay here" to dismiss. Both choices are remembered.

The budget bar at the bottom shows how much of your monthly Groceries budget is already used so you know exactly why the alert fired.

Feature 04 Voice Logger

Log Cash Expenses by Speaking

For cash purchases SpendWise can't read from SMS, just tap the mic and say what you spent. The AI extracts every field automatically.

1

Tap the microphone button on the home screen. The button pulses and shows "Listening…"

2

Speak your expense in plain English: "Paid 480 rupees for coffee at Barista" or "350 for tuk from Nugegoda".

3

Review the parsed card SpendWise shows the Merchant, Amount, Category, and Time it extracted. Tap any field to correct it.

4

Tap "Add to Feed" to save. The entry joins your expense feed tagged with a mic icon.

Feature 05 Subscription Detector

Find and Cancel Wasted Subscriptions

SpendWise scans 90 days of bank SMS history to find every recurring payment. Services you haven't used in 30+ days are flagged in red.

1

Open Subscriptions from the menu. The red banner at the top shows your total detected monthly waste in LKR.

2

Red rows = unused for 30+ days. Gold rows = renewing within 3 days. No colour = actively used keep these.

3

Tap "Cancel" on any red row. SpendWise opens the subscription management page of that service so you can cancel in one more tap.

4

Tap "Cancel All Waste" in the banner to bulk-cancel all flagged unused subscriptions at once.

Feature 06 OCR Receipt Scanner

Photograph Any Receipt Data Appears Instantly

Point your camera at any paper receipt. SpendWise reads the merchant name, date, line items, and VAT amount automatically then stores it in your encrypted digital vault.

1

Tap "Scan Receipt" in the SME menu. Your camera opens point it at any paper receipt or supplier invoice.

2

A green scanning frame appears with corner brackets. Hold steady for 2–3 seconds until the scan-line passes the entire receipt.

3

Review the extracted data merchant, date, total, and VAT amount appear in the result card. Tap any field to correct it.

4

Tap "Add to Feed" to log the expense, or "Add to Claim" to attach it to a reimbursement report. The receipt image is stored in your encrypted vault.

Feature 07 One-Click Reports

Generate IRD-Formatted Reports in One Click

Select a reporting period and categories SpendWise instantly builds a professional expense report formatted for Sri Lanka's Inland Revenue Department.

1

Go to Reports in the SME menu. Enter your business name and select the reporting period (month, quarter, or financial year).

2

Tick the expense categories you want to include: Groceries, Dining, Fuel, Accommodation, Software, Medical, or Training.

3

Tap "Generate Report". A professional itemised report appears instantly showing all transactions, category totals, and VAT claimable amount.

4

Tap "Download PDF" to save an IRD-formatted file, "Export CSV" to send to your accountant, or "Email Accountant" to send directly.

Corporate Features

Feature 08 Reimbursement Tracker

Submit Expense Claims Without Paperwork

All your company card transactions are tracked automatically via bank SMS. Review them, mark what needs reimbursing, and generate an IRD-formatted claim PDF in one tap.

1

Open Corporate → Reimbursements. All company card transactions appear automatically, categorised by type (Fuel, Meals, Accommodation, Travel).

2

Check the status badge on each row: Approved Submitted Pending

3

Tap "Generate IRD Claim PDF" a full reimbursement document is created instantly, including business purpose notes and VAT breakdown.

4

Tap "Submit to HR" to send the claim digitally to your Finance or HR team. No printing, no scanning, no email attachments needed.

Feature 09 B2B Retail Intelligence

Retail Analytics for Business Partners

Corporate retailer partners access anonymised consumer spending intelligence the only cross-retailer data source in Sri Lanka. All data is PDPA 2022 compliant.

1

Log in as a Retail Partner using your B2B portal credentials (separate from consumer login). Your catchment area and store name appear in the header.

2

Read the 4 KPI cards Weekly basket size, top spending category, peak shopping hours, and competitor switching rate. All figures update weekly.

3

Review the Category Demand chart see which product categories are trending in your area vs. the Colombo metro average. Use this to inform stock decisions.

4

Create a discount campaign configure a promotion and SpendWise delivers targeted alerts to nearby users who buy your product categories. Track results in the Campaign Cards.
