How OCR Scanning Works
1

Camera Capture or Email Forward

User photographs a receipt in-app, or forwards a supplier invoice email to their personal SpendWise inbox address. Both routes feed the exact same OCR pipeline.

2

OCR Text Extraction

Google Cloud Vision API processes the image handling skewed, rotated, or crumpled receipts. Optical Character Recognition extracts all text and reconstructs the tabular structure of itemised bills.

3

Field Parsing Merchant, Total, VAT

NLP post-processor identifies: store name (top of receipt), date, line items (tabular rows), sub-total, VAT amount, grand total, and VAT registration number if present.

4

Encrypted Digital Vault

Original image + extracted data stored in AES-256 encrypted cloud vault. Fully searchable by merchant, date range, amount, and VAT status. Tagged for reimbursement claims or year-end tax filing.

5

One-Click IRD Export

All VAT receipts export as a single PDF formatted for Sri Lanka's Inland Revenue Department making year-end tax filing straightforward for any SME owner.
