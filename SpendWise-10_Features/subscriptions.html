How subscription detection works
1

90-Day SMS History Scan

On first setup, SpendWise scans 90 days of stored bank SMS to find recurring charges at the same amount from the same merchant on a predictable cycle.

2

Recurring Pattern Recognition

Identifies charges with <3% variance recurring within a 28–32 day window (monthly) or 360–370 day window (annual) and adds them to the tracker.

3

Usage Data Cross-Reference

If a subscription app hasn't been opened in 30+ days, the service is flagged as "potentially unused" the red warning state you see above.

4

3-Day Renewal Alerts

Three days before every renewal, SpendWise pushes a notification with the charge amount tap to review or cancel before the money leaves your account.
